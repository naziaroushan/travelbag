Where next?

It was a cold month in 2017 and I was visiting family in New York. I was tired after traveling for almost a year and a half. Although I had bought tickets and got a visa to go to Turkey, I decided to stay and rest here. I have regretted that decision ever since I left Europe, and so it's at the top of my list.

Turkey

Turkey is a large peninsula that bridges the continents of Europe and Asia. Once the centre of the Ottoman Empire, the modern secular republic was established in the 1920s by nationalist leader Kemal Ataturk.
